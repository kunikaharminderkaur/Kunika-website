About Kunika
We bring you the purest spices and edible oils, crafted with care from farm to your kitchen.

From Our Barn to Your Home

Kunika is a family-run brand rooted in the heart of India. We specialize in premium-quality ground spices — Haldi, Dhaniya, Red Chilli, Cumin — and cold-pressed edible oils like Mustard & Peanut.

Every product is made using traditional methods, preserving the natural aroma, color, and health benefits. We believe in transparency — what you see is what you get.

Our Manufacturing Process
From farm to your kitchen — quality at every step
01 🌱

Farm Sourcing

We handpick the finest seeds, spices, and mustard from trusted local farms to ensure premium quality from the very beginning.

02 🔥

Roasting & Dehydrating

Modern roasting and dehydrating machines gently remove moisture while locking in natural flavors, essential oils, and vibrant colors.

03 ⚙️

Grinding & Pressing

Traditional grinding methods for spices and cold-pressing techniques for oils retain maximum nutrients and natural goodness.

04 🔬

Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality checks for purity, aroma, and taste to ensure you receive only the finest products.

05 🏷️

Hygienic Packaging

Our spices and oils are packed in state-of-the-art facilities to retain freshness, aroma, and nutritional value.

06 🚚

Delivered to Your Door

From our manufacturing unit to your kitchen — we ensure timely delivery of fresh, authentic products every time.
